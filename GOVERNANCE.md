# Governance

ProjectEX currently uses a maintainer-led model. The repository owner is the initial
maintainer and may appoint additional maintainers based on sustained, constructive
contributions and demonstrated care for security, licensing, and compatibility.

## Decisions

Routine changes use pull request consensus. Cross-cutting architecture, persistent
formats, public API, licensing, and governance changes require an ADR and maintainer
approval. When consensus is unavailable, the lead maintainer records the decision and
rationale publicly. Security incidents may be handled privately until disclosure.

## Maintainer responsibilities

Triage issues, review PRs, uphold conduct, protect release credentials, keep CI and
dependencies healthy, enforce provenance, publish releases, and communicate support
changes. Maintainers recuse themselves from conduct decisions involving themselves.

## Inactivity and succession

A maintainer inactive for six months may move to emeritus status after private contact.
Project ownership should be transferred to an active trusted maintainer or organization
if the lead can no longer maintain releases. No contributor is guaranteed a role based
solely on commit count.
